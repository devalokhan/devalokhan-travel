MailChimp

To integrate a Subscription box with MailChimp you just need to create an account with MailChimp account, obtain your unique MailChimp API Key and List ID, and then add it to the code in your template.

To get your API Key: https://us4.admin.mailchimp.com/account/api/2

To get your List ID: http://kb.mailchimp.com/article/how-can-i-find-my-list-id1

Check This flowing Image

Replace those marked area with your "Api Key" and "List id" , this should do the trick to intrigate your mailchimp account with your template subscription form.
